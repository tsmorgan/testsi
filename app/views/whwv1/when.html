When to self-isolate

Advice about when to self-isolate, what circumstances to self-isolate and the law about self-isolation

  • You have been in close contact with someone who has Covid-19 like symptoms or who has tested positive for Covid-19
  • Someone in your support bubble has tested positive for Covid-19
  • You live with someone who has Covid-19 like symptoms or who has tested positive for Covid-19
  • You have symptoms that might mean you have Covid-19
  • You have tested positive for Covid-19
  • You have been told to self-isolate by NHS Test and Trace
  • You have been told to self isolate by the NHS Covid-19 App
  • The NHS Covid-19 App has advised you that you have visited a venue whilst some with Covid-19 was there
