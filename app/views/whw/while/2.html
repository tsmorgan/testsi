How to look after yourself when Self-isolating

Staying at home and self-isolating can be difficult, stressful or inconvenient but by self-isolating you're saving lives. There are things you can do now to help you if you need to Self-isolate in the future.

Getting food, medication and other supplies

Food:

  • set up an online account with your supermarket
  • have a small supply of basic essentials with long shelf lives ready

Medication:

  • contact your local council and ask if your medication can be delivered by your local community hub
  • ask your local pharmacy if they can deliver your medication
  • sign up online to have your prescription delivered by Boots, Pharmacy2U or Well
Information:

While you're ill, ask a friend, family member or neighbour to check up on you. Arrange a regular call or talk though a doorway (not face to face) so they can check how you're doing.
