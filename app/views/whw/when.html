When to self-isolate

Advice about when to self-isolate, what circumstances to self-isolate and the law about self-isolation

  • You know someone who has Covid symptoms or who has tested positive for Covid-19
  • You have symptoms that might mean you have Covid-19 or have got it again
  • You have requested or taken a Covid-19
  • You have been told to self-isolate by NHS Test and Trace
  • You have been told to self-isolate by the NHS Covid-19 app
  • You have been received an NHS Covid-19 app venue alert
